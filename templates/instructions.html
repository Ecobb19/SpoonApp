How to Play

Get ready for some epic spoon action! Here are the instructions for the Spoon Assassins game:

  1. Players are divided into teams, ready to unleash their spoon-wielding skills.
  2. Each team is given a shiny, magical spoon to wield as their weapon of choice.
  3. Players must stealthily eliminate opponents by tapping them with the spoon, leaving them in awe of their spoon-fu mastery.
  4. Eliminated players must gracefully hand over their spoon to the player who skillfully outspooned them.
  5. The game continues with intense spoon battles, filled with laughter and suspense, until only one player remains victorious, crowned as the ultimate Spoon Assassin!

How the App Works

Are you ready to become a Spoon Assassin? Here's how the app works:

  • A game admin creates an account to create and monitor games.
  • When the admin creates a game, it is assigned a unique GameID.
  • Players use the GameID and a custom login to join the game.
  • Once the admin starts the game, each player is assigned a target.
  • Players must assassinate their target by any means necessary.
  • After eliminating their target, players submit the confirmation on their page.
  • Upon confirmation, players are automatically assigned a new target.
  • The game continues until there is only one player left standing.
  • The admin can view the winner of the game.
